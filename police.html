Stratford home invasion suspects arrested after similar incidents in New York, New Jersey

Officers arrested two Stratford home invasion suspects tied to similar incidents across Connecticut, New York and New Jersey

Ryeim Robinson, 18, of Newark, N.J. and an unidentified minor are in police custody, both of whom were involved in a home invasion in Stratford, the town's police department said.

On July 15, Stratford police went to a home in the Lordship section of town for a report of a home invasion. Police said they learned that multiple suspects went into an occupied residence during the early morning and stole a vehicle and "other valuables."

The suspects also removed registration plates from other vehicles in the neighborhood, police said.

A police investigation involving state and local officials from Connecticut, New Jersey and New York identified Robinson as the main suspect in the Lordship incident.
